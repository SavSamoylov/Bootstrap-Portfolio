About Me

Hello all! My name is Saveliy, and I am happy to be going through this coding bootcamp with the rest of you.

I was born in Russia, but have spent the majority of my life here in the United States. I enjoy watching sitcoms, listening to all kinds of podcasts, and the idea of being fit... though that one is a little harder to keep up with.

I have some previous experience with using HTML and CSS, but I never thought that I could be a legitimate Web Developer because learning Javascript and other backend languages seemed like it would be too difficult. I now realize that I spent far too long holding myself back with doubt. I am attending this Bootcamp in order to secure a better job in a career field that I am passionate and excited about.
